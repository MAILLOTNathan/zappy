To explore Amber world, you can do many things !

If you want to move yourself in the world you can press :
    - [W] -> to move forward
    - [S] -> to move backward
    - [A] -> to move left
    - [D] -> to move right

To look around yourself, you must use the [mouse].

And to switch between the camera and the free mouse pointer,
you may use the [C] key.

Now let's discover how much you can configure you experience !
